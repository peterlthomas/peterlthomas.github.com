Gallery / California 2004 / Tamarancho

Tamarancho

This week I headed a bit further north for some Marin County riding in Tamarancho. A few people from MTBR.com were heading out there, so I thought I'd tag along. If you like switchbacks then you'll love Tamarancho, there was 40 on the trail we did! Great ride!

Mt Tam and Repack are somewhere around here, so no doubt I'll be back soon!
