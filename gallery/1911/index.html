Big Sur

We found this little restaurant tucked away on the side of a cliff top in Big Sur. Its one of those places that looks like nothing from the road, but when you get up there.... well thats a different story.

We got to eat with a 180 degree view of the sun setting into the Pacific Ocean - its was incredible. I haven't touched the colours in Photoshop ... this is pretty much exactly how it looked.
